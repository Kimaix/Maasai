About Me

I'm Sherath Kimai, a proud Maasai tech innovator driven by the dream of merging Maasai culture with cutting-edge technology.

Through creativity, code, and community, I build tools and digital experiences that empower, educate, and celebrate Maasai identity.

My work reimagines how technology can carry the heartbeat of Maasai tradition into the digital age — authentic, inspiring, and proudly Maasai.

Vision

To build a digital future that reflects the Maasai people — a space where innovation respects our culture, creativity drives empowerment, and technology speaks our native rhythm.

I believe in tech for identity, not just convenience. My goal is to craft systems that honor Maasai roots while unlocking new possibilities for our future.
